How do I become an partner?
If you want your music to be played on our radio, all you have to do is send a link to your music and verify ownership of it. Send us a message describing your request and we will contact you for further work.
Do I need to license my music?
If you're in the business of distributing original music then you probably have official resources where you share it with your fans. Links to these resources may be compatible for rotation.
Do you pay royalties?
Our radio is free of charge. We do not pay any remuneration to our authors. Everything that you provide us, you do on your own.
Will I have to pay for rotation?
At this point in time, we do not charge for the rotation of author's music. But if the number of orders is too high, we will probably introduce some restrictions or additional fees.
