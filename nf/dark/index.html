Society

We present to your attention our development in the field of social networks Atlas.Network. This application is designed to organize communication on the Internet. With its help, anyone can organize their personal space online. Maintain private channels and publish your ideas. Communicate with other users in messenger mode by sharing media content. Play built-in games and so on.

Atlas.Network contains built-in artificial intelligence algorithms implemented as virtual users. ChatGPT, Weather service and other high-tech services. To use them, just add such users (bots) to your friends list and start a text chat with them. We also accept third-party services for development as public assistants for your business.

Surround Sound

We have developed a system Atlas.Music for broadcasting music in volumetric format via the Internet. Having a multi-channel audio system in your home arsenal, you can connect to our service and listen to music in high quality in Dolby Digital Surround 5.1 format (BlueRay).
